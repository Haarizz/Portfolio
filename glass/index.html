target The Problem

Retail businesses running multiple branches juggle separate tools for stock, billing, accounting and payroll. Data drifts out of sync, financial reporting is manual, and there's no single source of truth across outlets.

spa The Solution

A unified ERP where every module shares one data model. Inventory, sales, purchase, accounting and payroll flow into a single financial ledger, with branch-level isolation and role-based access.
